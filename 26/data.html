Invariant pT distributions for |y| < 0.5. Errors listed are statistical only. A power-law fit to the p+p spectrum yielded dN/dy = 0.259 +/- 0.002 (stat) +/- 0.039 (sys) and <pT> = 0.616 +/- 0.002 (stat) +/- 0.062 (sys) GeV/c. An exponential fit in (mT-m0) to the Au+Au data yielded dN/dy = 5.4 +/- 0.1 (stat) +/- 1.2 (sys), and <pT> = 0.83 +/- 0.01(stat) +/- 0.10 (sys) GeV/c.
