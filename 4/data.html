Numerical values of HBT parameters

This page contains numbers for the pion correlations from the first STAR HBT paper. They are Coulomb corrected (5 fm Gaussian source assumed), corrected for merging effects ("bad systematic" for STAR-HBT insiders), and corrected for finite-momentum-resolution effects, as discussed in the paper.
Systematic errors are estimated by the size of the merging correction and the effect of varying the source size used in the Coulomb correction by +/- 1 fm.
Below, statistical uncertainties are followed by the systematic ones.
